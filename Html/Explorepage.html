Travel Tips & Advices ✈️

Best Time to Visit 🕐

  • Spring (April-June) - Pleasant weather, blooming gardens, and fewer crowds.
  • Summer (July-August) - Great for hiking, festivals, and lakes—but it's peak tourist season.
  • Autumn (September-October) - Colorful landscapes and ideal for wine tasting.
  • Winter (December-March) - Perfect for skiing, Christmas markets, and cozy vibes.

Getting Around 🚆

  • 🚆 Trains - Fast, clean, and reliable.
  • 🚌 Buses - Good for regional travel and cheaper than trains.
  • 🚗 Car rental - Great for scenic drives, especially through the Alps or countryside.
  • 🚊 Vienna public transport - Efficient U-Bahn, trams, and buses. A day pass is a good deal!

Money & Costs 💶

  • 💵 Currency - Euro (€)
  • 🪙 Tipping - Usually 5-10% in restaurants. Round up in taxis.
  • 🏧 ATMs - Widely available and often offer better rates than exchange booths.

Language & Communication 🗣️

  • 🏳️ Language - German is the official language.
  • 🇬🇧 English is commonly spoken in cities and tourist areas.
  • 💬 Useful phrases:
    • 👋 Hallo! - Hello
    • 🙏 Danke! - Thank you
    • ❓ Sprechen Sie Englisch? - Do you speak English?
    • 🚻 Wo ist die Toilette? - Where is the toilet?
